Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my site will be to advertise my fictional white water rafting website as a fun option for vacations and other adventures. I want to pull in potential customers with a nice design and the promise of a positive experience.

Audience

My audience is primarily families looking for a fun activity to go on together. I am focused on families that are looking for something fun to do that will bond them and bring them closer together. I imagine they would primarily access the site on desktop so that they can all look at it together.
